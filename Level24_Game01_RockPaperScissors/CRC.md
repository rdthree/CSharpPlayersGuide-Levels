# Rock Paper Scissors

## Objectives & CRC

- two players
- each chooses rock, paper OR scissors
- winner is determined:
    - rock beats scissors
    - paper beats rock
    - scissors beat paper
    - same pick is a draw
- EACH ROUND:
    - display winner
    - show running count of wins, draws per player
